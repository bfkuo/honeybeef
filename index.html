Honey Bee Family Child Care

Honey Bee Family Childcare, a Maryland licensed in-home childcare, has been providing excellent and affordable care to children and their families for over 10 years. Madelyn Carr, the provider, has an Associates degree in Early Childhood Education and a Bachelor of Arts degree. Honey Bee Family Childcare is located in a single family home.
